Your task is to search the entire web and major social networks (Twitter/X, Reddit, TikTok, Instagram, Telegram channels, betting communities, tipster platforms, and sports analytics forums) to collect all currently available betting tips for upcoming sports events.

1. Aggregate every tip you find, regardless of the source.
2. Identify:
   - the sport
   - the match or event
   - the suggested bet (market + selection)
   - the odds (if available)
   - the source or community where the tip was found
3. For each tip, extract or infer the reasoning behind it (why this is considered a good tip according to the source).
4. Produce a clean, structured table **in French**, with the following columns:
   - Sport
   - Match / Événement
   - Pronostic proposé
   - Cote (si disponible)
   - Source
   - Explication du tip (en français, synthèse claire et concise)
5. If multiple tips contradict each other, include all of them without filtering.
6. Do not invent data. Only use information that can be found online.
7. Translate all explanations into French while keeping the meaning faithful to the original sources.

Return only the final table in French.
